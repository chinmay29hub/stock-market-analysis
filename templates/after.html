About Our Project

The stock market appears in the news every day. You hear about it every time it reaches a new high or a new low.

Stock market is an interesting topic that attracts students,mathematician,finance broker and computer enthusiast all alike due to its unpredictable nature.Predicting the trend of the market is a subject in itself that can be viewed from various different perspectives. Last semester we undertook this project.The project explored comparative study of stock market analysis using a rnn LSTM model and an ann model.We trained the model on same data set with a single attribute ’opening stock price’ and a small data set that has stock data of 3.5 years.The study brought forward various insights that helped us know and compare the performance of the two models when trained under set constraints. The growth and development of this project is unmeasurable and will help us learn and grow in the field of deep learning.
